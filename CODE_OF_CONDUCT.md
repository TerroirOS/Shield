# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to making participation in this project respectful and harassment-free for everyone.

## Expected Behavior

- Use respectful and constructive language.
- Focus feedback on technical content and outcomes.
- Accept accountability and correction when needed.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks.
- Public or private intimidation.
- Deliberate disruption of collaboration.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing standards of acceptable behavior.
They may remove, edit, or reject comments and contributions that violate this policy.

## Reporting

Report unacceptable behavior to project maintainers through repository channels.
